Crystal Collector

Greetings, Crystal Collector! Are you ready to set out on a full day of seeking out those precious minerals? Does the idea of a large haul of shiny gems excite you? Well, you're in the right place!

At the beginning of each day you are given a bag for your crystals. But, be careful! Each bag can only carry a certain weight. But you want to get your money's worth, right? Of course you do--crystal collecting is hard work, after all. That means your goal, brave Crystal Collector, is to fill your bag to the absolute brim and make your way home from the mines. If your bag rips, however, your day is wasted and you get no crystals to add to your collection.

Each of the four types of crystals below are a different weight, but you will not know how much each type of crystal weighs until you have picked it up for the first time that day and put it in your bag.

Everything clear? Yes? Then off you go on your adventure! Click on a crystal to put it in your bag and begin your mission.
